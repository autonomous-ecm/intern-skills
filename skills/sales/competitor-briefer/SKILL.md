# Competitor Briefer

## Description
Create battle cards and competitive intelligence: quick comparisons with competitors, prepare objection handling for sales.

## Trigger
- User needs competitor information for a sales call
- User says "battle card", "competitor", "comparison", "objection handling"
- A customer mentions they are evaluating a competitor

## Instructions

### When to Activate
- Prepare before a call when you know the customer is using/evaluating a competitor
- Customer asks "Why should I choose you instead of X?"
- Create/update a battle card for a competitor
- Train the sales team on competitive positioning
- A deal was lost to a competitor → post-mortem

### Battle Card Structure
1. **Competitor Overview**: Who they are, what they do, target market
2. **Quick Compare**: Feature comparison table
3. **Our Strengths**: Where we are stronger (with proof)
4. **Their Strengths**: Where they are stronger (be honest)
5. **Common Objections**: "They're cheaper", "They're more well-known"
6. **Objection Responses**: Scripts to address each objection
7. **Landmines**: Questions to ask the customer that expose competitor weaknesses
8. **Win Stories**: Case studies where we won against this competitor

### Objection Handling Framework
```
1. ACKNOWLEDGE: "That's right, I understand that concern..."
2. REFRAME: "However, what's more important is..."
3. PROVE: "Customer X also thought the same, but after using our solution..."
4. CTA: "Let me show you specifically how..."
```

### Rules
- NEVER badmouth the competitor — focus on your own value
- Information must be accurate — wrong information = instant loss of trust
- Update battle cards monthly (competitors change quickly)
- Train on objection handling, do not just read — role play
- Win/loss analysis: track reasons for winning/losing against each competitor

