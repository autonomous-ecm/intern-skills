# Social Media Planner

## Description
Plan social media strategy: content calendar, post idea suggestions, scheduling, and engagement analysis.

## Trigger
- User needs to plan social media
- User says "social media", "posting schedule", "content calendar", "Facebook", "Instagram", "TikTok"
- User needs post ideas for the week/month

## Instructions

### When to Activate
- Create a weekly/monthly content calendar
- Brainstorm post ideas
- Schedule posts at optimal times
- Analyze best time to post
- Plan campaigns for events/promotions

### Platforms & Best Practices
- **Facebook**: 1-2 posts/day, video + carousel perform well, 10:00 AM & 8:00 PM
- **Instagram**: 1 post/day + 3-5 stories, aesthetic visuals, Reels prioritized
- **TikTok**: 1-3 videos/day, trending sounds, hook in the first 3 seconds
- **LinkedIn**: 2-3 posts/week, professional tone, long-form text
- **Twitter/X**: 3-5 tweets/day, real-time, threads

### Content Pillars (template)
- **Educational** (30%): Tips, how-to, industry insights
- **Entertaining** (25%): Memes, behind-the-scenes, team stories
- **Promotional** (25%): Product features, offers, launches
- **Engagement** (20%): Polls, Q&A, user-generated content

### Process
1. Define monthly goals (awareness, engagement, conversion)
2. Choose appropriate content pillars
3. Brainstorm ideas by pillar
4. Schedule: platform + date/time + content type
5. Prepare assets (copy, visual direction, hashtags)

### Rules
- 80/20 rule: 80% value content, 20% promotional
- Every post must have a CTA (even a soft one: "What do you think?")
- Hashtags: 3-5 for FB, 15-30 for IG, 3-5 for TikTok
- Respond to comments within 1-2 hours
- A/B test: try 2 versions for important posts
