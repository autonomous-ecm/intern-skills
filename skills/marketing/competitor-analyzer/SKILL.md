# Competitor Analyzer

## Description
Analyze competitors: compare products, pricing, marketing strategy, USP, and market positioning.

## Trigger
- User needs to analyze competitors
- User says "competitor", "competition", "comparison", "benchmark", "market analysis"
- User wants to understand the competitive landscape

## Instructions

### When to Activate
- Analyze a specific competitor
- Compare products/services with competitors
- Research competitor pricing strategy
- Analyze competitor marketing/content strategy
- Identify competitive advantage / USP

### Analysis Framework
- **Product**: Features, quality, UX, pricing
- **Marketing**: Channels, messaging, content strategy, ad spend
- **Brand**: Positioning, reputation, reviews, NPS
- **Digital**: Website traffic, SEO rankings, social following
- **Business**: Revenue, growth, funding, team size

### Process
1. Identify: List the top 3-5 competitors (direct + indirect)
2. Collect: Gather information from websites, social media, reviews, news
3. Compare: Evaluate using the framework
4. Analyze: Find strengths, weaknesses, opportunities, threats
5. Recommend: Suggest ways to differentiate

### Rules
- Analyze objectively, data-driven — no bias
- Distinguish direct competitors (same product) from indirect (same need)
- Update at least quarterly (the competitive landscape changes fast)
- Focus on actionable insights, not just data collection
- Always conclude with: "We should..." (next actions)
